How to use Stage Mode

                
            

            

                
Getting started

                
• Go to Playlist (top right) to add or load your songs first.

                
• Once loaded, your lyrics appear here ready to scroll.

                
Playback

                
• Tap Start to begin scrolling. Tap Pause to stop mid-song.

                
• Tap Reset to snap back to the first line instantly.

                
• On a keyboard: Space = Start / Pause, R = Reset.

                
Adjusting on the fly

                
• Drag the lyric window up or down at any time to reposition.

                
• Dragging while playing pauses scroll, then resumes when you release.

                
Settings

                
• Use Settings to adjust scroll speed, font size and line spacing.

                
• Tap Save to song in Settings to remember these values per song.

                
• Enable 4-beat count-in for a 4-second countdown overlay before scrolling starts.

                
Navigation

                
• Use ⏮ ⏭ buttons to jump between songs in your playlist.

                
• The Notes button shows chords or cues saved for the current song.
